Mingming He (贺明明)


I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at VGL@USC ICT from 2019 to 2021. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Computer Science & Engineering, HKUST in 2018, supervised by Pedro V. Sander. Before that, I received my M.S. degree under the supervision of Kun Zhou and B.E. degree from Zhejiang University in 2014 and 2011, respectively. I was a research intern in the Visual Computing Group of MSRA supervised by Jing Liao and Lu Yuan during 2017. My research interest is Computer Graphics and Computer Vision, mainly focusing on computational photography and visual synthesis.
